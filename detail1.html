Eau De Forte

Alcohol-Free Fragrance - Fresh and Intense Notes

$100 / 100 mL

Description

The legendary freshness of Sauvage strengthened and prolonged by the power of water. A strength made possible by an innovative alcohol-free formula exclusive to Dior. Inspired by the symbolic power of water, Francis Kurkdjian, Dior Perfume Creative Director, imagined Sauvage Eau Forte, an olfactory composition as fresh as an eau de toilette and as intense as a parfum. This creation explores the uncharted territory of a fresh and spicy fougère. Like a waterfall, Sauvage Eau Forte delivers freshness and intensity. An aqueous formula mixes with a fragrant oily formula to create this highly concentrated eau with a milky hue. Strengthened by the freshness of water that "drenches" the ingredients, the scent of the fragrance is composed around notes of cool spices, bleached lavender and musky woods. Sauvage Eau Forte, the power of water.
